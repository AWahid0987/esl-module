Features

End-to-End FBR Digital Invoicing

Automated invoice data submission to Pakistan FBR system under SRO 709 with secure, real-time synchronization from Odoo.

Complete FBR Reference Data Setup

Preconfigured UOM, Provinces, Document Types, Buyer Types, Sales Scenarios and Reasons to align Odoo master data with FBR requirements.

Automated Tax Compliance Management

Tax calculation as per FBR rules, including province-wise sales tax rates, buyer type and document type based tax behaviour.

Sales Tax Return Preparation

Structured sales-side data for monthly/quarterly FBR sales tax returns with clean mapping from Odoo invoices and credit notes.

Purchase Tax Management

Manage input tax on purchases inside Odoo and reconcile it with output tax to support accurate return working and adjustments.

FBR Rate & Rule Configuration

Centralized configuration of FBR tax rates, applicability rules and effective dates so that changes in law are easily updated in the system.

SRO & Item Serial Management

Maintain Statutory Regulatory Orders (SROs) and SRO-wise item serial numbers to correctly apply special tax treatments and exemptions.

Compliance Reporting & Audit Trail

Dedicated FBR compliance reports, document classification, buyer type breakdowns and audit trail of FBR submissions directly from Odoo.
